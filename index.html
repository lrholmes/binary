Voice

Use your voice and make decisions with words like 'yes' and 'no'.

Hands

Use swiping motions with your hands to flick the cards towards your decision.

Head

Use shaking and nodding gestures to make your decision.

Colour

Use green or red coloured objects to indicate your decision.

Guitar

Use the high and low notes on a guitar to make your decision.

Sway

Lean to indicate the direction you'd like the card to go.

Eyes

Use your eyes to indicate the direction of the cards.
